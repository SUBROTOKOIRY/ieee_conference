IEEE JSS STU International Conference on “Emerging Technologies in Electronics and Green Energy 2025”

Overview of the Conference

IEEE JSS STU International Conference on “Emerging Technologies in Electronics and Green Energy 2025” (IEEE JSS STU ICETEG - 2025) has been planned to be organized on 21th and 22nd February 2025 by JSS S&TU (SJCE), JSS Technical Institutions Campus, Mysuru, Karnataka, India. IEEE JSS STU ICETEG – 2025 is a premier international conference and is technically sponsored by IEEE Bangalore Section organized by three circuit branch departments, Electronics and Communication, Electronics and Instrumentation and Electronics and Electrical Engineering. The theme for ICETEG – 2025 is to promote the most upcoming innovations and advancement in various fields of engineering and technology bringing together academia and industry. This conference provides an excellent opportunity and benefit for faculty members, research scholars, students and industry delegates to present, demonstrate and discuss innovative research developments in the fields of electronics, communication, green energy, computing technology and biomedical applications.
